Our Vision Is To Provide Good Food To Students Away From Home

We are Delhi Delight

Delhi Cafe Delight, located within the AIT Pune campus, is a dedicated culinary establishment focused on providing high-quality snacks and other food items to the student community. Specializing in quick, yet delicious offerings, the cafe caters to the diverse tastes and preferences of its patrons. The menu is thoughtfully curated to meet the needs of students looking for a convenient and satisfying culinary experience. With a commitment to professionalism and a welcoming ambiance.
